About

Purpose

Our goal is to provide some visualization of country happiness and life satisfaction for some basic analysis.

Data

All data comes from the compiled World Happiness Report (2005-2021). The World Happiness Report is published by the United Nations Sustainable Development Solutions Network. It includes statistics on national happiness based on citizen ratings of their own lives. It also includes various other quality of life factors. The majority of their data comes from the Gallup World Poll.
